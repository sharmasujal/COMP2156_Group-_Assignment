SDLC stands for Software Development Life Cycle. It's a structured process used by development teams to design, develop, test, and deploy software efficiently.
Key features of SDLC:

1.Structured approach: Organizes software development into phases.
2.Requirements management: Focuses on gathering and managing project needs.
3.Iterative development: Supports continuous improvement through cycles.
4.Quality assurance: Emphasizes testing for bugs and errors.
5.Documentation: Encourages comprehensive documentation at each stage.
6.Flexibility: Adapts to project and organizational needs.
